Hindi Font Style Generator respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This extension is designed to help users generate stylish Hindi text, preview Hindi font styles, and download supported font resources directly within Chrome.

The extension operates entirely within your browser. User-entered text is used only to generate real-time font previews and create downloadable outputs such as PNG images or supported font files.
